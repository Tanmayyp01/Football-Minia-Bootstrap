Over his years at Barcelona, Messi set numerous records and became the club's all-time leading goal scorer. He won numerous domestic and international titles, including multiple UEFA Champions League titles and La Liga titles.

Messi is known for his exceptional skills, agility, vision, and precise dribbling. Messi's left foot is particularly renowned for its accuracy and power.
